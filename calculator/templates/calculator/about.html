About Retirement Planner

A multi-phase retirement planning tool built to fill a gap in existing financial calculators.

Why This Tool Exists

Most retirement calculators assume a simple two-phase model: you save until retirement, then you withdraw. But real retirement planning is more nuanced. What about phased retirement where you work part-time? What about different withdrawal strategies in your 60s versus your 80s? What about planning for healthcare costs that escalate in late retirement?

This tool was built because existing calculators didn't support multi-phase planning. It models:

  • Phase 1 (Accumulation): Traditional saving years with compound growth
  • Phase 2 (Phased Retirement): Transition years with reduced income and partial contributions
  • Phase 3 (Active Retirement): Early retirement with higher withdrawal rates for travel and activities
  • Phase 4 (Late Retirement): Later years with adjusted spending patterns and healthcare considerations

It provides detailed, phase-specific projections so you can see exactly how your portfolio evolves through each stage of life.
